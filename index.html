Saehoon Kim (김세훈)

Kakao Brain. Pangyo, Seongnam. Korea.

I am an AI research scientist at KakaoBrain. Prior to joining KakaoBrain, I had led a machine learning team at AITRICS (medical AI startup).

I finished my Ph.D. and B.S. degrees in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at POSTECH. I had spent several months for internship programs at MSRA and MSR. During my Ph.D., I had studied both randomized and learning algorithms for approximate nearest neighbor search.

My research interests lie in continual learning, Bayesian deep learning, locality-sensitive hashing, and machine learning for healthcare.
